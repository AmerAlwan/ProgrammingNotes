seven segment display (common Anode)

        

activity

1) Assemble the circuit on the breadboard. (Be careful not to bend the pins on the 7-segment display). Be sure to connect power to pins 3 and 11 through two 220Ω resistors.

2) Touch the ground wire to each pin on the 7-segment display (except pins 3 and 11 for power, and 4 and 7 for decimal point) and observe the 7-segment display.
