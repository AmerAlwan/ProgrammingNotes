SPOUT BOT

SPOUT is a simple "robot" based on the beetle-bot concept that you can find pretty much anywhere on the internet. This specific design can be found on Khan Academy. It is a very simple circuit design and can be assembled fairly quickly.

PARTS

Make sure that your kit contains all of these materials. DO NOT START if you are missing parts!

  • 1 - AA Battery Holder
  • 2 - DC Brushed Hobby Motor
  • 2 - LED
  • 2 - 100Ω resistor
  • 2 - SPDT Limit Switch
  • 2 - SPDT Slide Switch
  • 1 - Small Paper Clip
  • 3 - Large Paper Clip
  • 2 - Bottle Cap
  • 25cm Wire
