arduino final project

Your job is to expand upon what you have learned so far. You are to create a game of rock-paper-scissor. Your game should have two players; player 1 is the computer, player 2 is a person. Beyond this you can create the game any way that you would like. Your mark will be based on the components that you use, and the complexity of your programming. Ideally you want to include the use of as many components as possible; LED's, RGB LED, potentiometer, buttons, buzzer, randomization, arrays, selection structures, and repetition structures. The rules for playing the game need to be written down but do not need to be connected to the working of the program (think of it as a plan).
